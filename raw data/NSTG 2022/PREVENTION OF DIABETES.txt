PREVENTION OF DIABETES 
Prevention of diabetes
Obesity and physical inactivity are the major modifiable risk factors, and
should be avoided/corrected
Onset of diabetes can be delayed in people at high risk by active lifestyle
modification
Lifestyle modification should be the cornerstone of preventative strategies in
the following categories of people:
・ Age > 45 years
・ Overweight and obesity (BMI > 25 kg/m2)
・ Physical inactivity
・ First degree relatives with diabetes
・ Previous gestational diabetes
・ Previously identified IGT or IFG
・ Dyslipidaemia
・ Hypertension
