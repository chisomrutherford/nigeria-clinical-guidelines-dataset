RHEUMATOID ARTHRITIS
Introduction
A disease of unknown aetiology; autoimmune mechanisms and immune
complex formation have been implicated. Usually begins in early adult life
and affects females more frequently.

Patients rarely complain of pain from temporomandibular joint (TMJ) but
clinical examination shows TMJ involvement in 50% of cases. Limitation of
mouth opening; softness, crepitus, referred pain, and tenderness on biting.
Severe disability is unusual
